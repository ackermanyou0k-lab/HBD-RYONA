Happy Birthday, Ryona 🎉🤍

It’s crazy to think we’ve known each other for almost 5 years now—maybe we’ve been real, truly close friends for about 2 years. And honestly, those years mean so much to me. High school wouldn’t have been the same without you and all our memories together.

I know things haven’t been the same lately, and I’m really sorry for what happened. I truly regret it. If I could go back, I would definitely do things better. You and our friendships matter more to me than you probably think.

On your special day, I just want to wish you all the happiness, success, and good things in the world. I hope every dream you have slowly comes true, because you really deserve it.

I also hope that one day, we can be close again like before—laughing, being silly, playing, and spending time together with everyone. Even if life takes us in different directions, I believe we’re both trying our best to build a great future.

Thank you for being part of my life and for being one of my best friends. I love you—not in a romantic way, but in a “you’re really important to me and I care about you a lot” kind of way.

By the way… do you remember when I gave you an eraser last year? 😅 I was so confused about what gift to give you back then. Plus, I had that tiny little crush and everything was complicated. So yeah… the eraser actually meant I wanted to start over—to erase the bad things and be friends like before. Not because it was cheap, okay 😭 I just didn’t know what else to do at that time.

And I still don’t know why you gave me perfume on my birthday… do I really smell that bad? 😂 Wow. Rude. Just kidding.

This year, I was super serious about choosing something for you. I even stressed about it 😤 So I made you a birthday website, got you a George Russell hat, and a sunflower keychain. I chose sunflowers because they mean friendship and loyalty. That’s how I see you—and that’s what I hope we’ll always be.

I hope we can stay best friends for a long, long time, with you and everyone, through everything life throws at us.

Happy Birthday once again, Gea 🤍

Always cheering for you.
